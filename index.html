Owner Onboarding

Everything you'll need
to get started.

Once you're ready to move forward, here's a checklist of what we'll need from you. Our team handles all filings and coordination — you just need to provide the documents.

1
Maricopa County Rental Registration
Register your property as a rental at mcassessor.maricopa.gov. Search your address, select "Register Rental" and follow prompts.
2
TPT License
Transaction Privilege Tax license obtained via aztaxes.gov. Create an account, select "Register a New Business" and complete the JT1 application. Allow 5–10 days.
3
Local City Permit
Phoenix, Paradise Valley, and Scottsdale each require a local STR permit. LuxeKey handles this on your behalf with a signed authorization form.
4
Insurance
Homeowners policy with $1M minimum liability coverage. LuxeKey LLC must be listed as additionally insured. Notify your agent the property is used as an STR.
5
Direct Deposit Authorization
ACH direct deposit form to receive monthly disbursements. Completed via our secure onboarding portal.
6
W-9 & TPT Form 285
W-9 for annual 1099 reporting. Form 285-PMC authorizes LuxeKey to file your TPT remittance. Both completed via secure link.
7
Trust Account Reserve
A reserve of $500–$3,000 held in the trust account to cover vendor expenses between rental payments. Amount depends on your service volume.
8
Property Readiness
Property must be furnished and ready for occupancy. Once complete, your listing will be live within 14 days of signing. LuxeKey can assist with furnishing and setup if needed.
